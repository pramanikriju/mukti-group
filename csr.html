Corporate Social Responsibility

Since its inception, Mukti Group has actively participated in CSR and philanthropic activities by undertaking various social and educational initiatives for the benefit of the under- privileged and street children in association with ‘Help Us Help Them’, a non-profit organisation. The Group is committed to being a responsible corporate citizen and is focused on extending prosperity to the wider external community. A top priority of Mukti Group’s CSR agenda is to improve the living conditions of the underprivileged and to make a positive difference in their lives. A number of focused initiatives have been implemented to bring about general health awareness and to improve the level of education, particularly for street children, with a philosophy of igniting young minds and shaping the future of young India.


Few of our participation in social causes are:

  • Sponsoring the True Puja Spirit awards during Durga Puja in association with The Telegraph and CESC.
  • The Group also sponsored for a noble cause christened ‘Kolkata Gives’- a platform for the coming together of Kolkata’s largest donors and NGOs’ - the first-of-its-kind initiative in the City of Joy. The event was structured around the largest collection of Kolkata’s high net worth individuals and some of the most credible NGOs shortlisted on the basis of their experience, sectoral potential, professionalism and resource management under one roof.
