About Us

Who We are

Mukti Group is a respected and renowned business conglomerate with operations spread across Eastern India in the areas of Real Estate (residential and commercial), Hospitality (five-star hotel) and Entertainment (mall and multiplex).

History of the Group

Mukti Group was formed in 1984 and played a key role in the growth of real estate in Eastern India by developing several state-of-the-art residential and commercial properties. In 2009, the Group ventured into the entertainment and hospitality sector through ‘Mukti World’ - a sought-after entertainment and dining hub. In 2010, the Group launched its own multiplex brand - ‘London Paris Multiplex’. In 2012, the Company forayed into the hospitality sector through Kolkata’s first international five-star hotel ‘Park Plaza’ in a management tie-up with the Radisson Hotel Group. In 2018, ‘Park Plaza’ was upgraded to ‘Radisson’.

Chairman Speak

It is a matter of pride for me to introduce Mukti Group, one of the prestigious institutions in Eastern India. Since the Group’s first business venture in 1984, it has been instrumental in its contribution to the development of real estate in Eastern India. After successfully launching several residential and commercial projects, the Group forayed into Hospitality and Entertainment.
Kolkata provides an access to North East India and with initiatives to promote industry and tourism by the Government of West Bengal over the past few years, the potential for business and leisure travellers has increased significantly. Considering this, many of the world’s best known hospitality brands are looking to the East. After its strong presence in East India, the Group is focusing on spreading its wings to the Northern and Western parts of India.

The Group plans to contribute to the development of infrastructure of international standards in the areas of education, health care and entertainment. Above all, we want to be a responsible corporate. My family and businesses are actively involved in the development of under-privileged families and strive to provide a safe and happy work environment for our team-members. I thank everyone who has been a part of our journey and look forward to your continued support and good wishes.

With my very best regards,
R.K. Gupta,
Chairman, Mukti Group
